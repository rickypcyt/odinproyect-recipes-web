Garfield's Lasagna

Garfield loves lasagna more than anyone, and we developed this lasagna recipe drawing inspiration from the famous cartoon cat. As a result, we enjoyed a creamy, filling, and tasty dish. Lasagna is Garfield’s favorite dish, and we love it too. It’s filling, hearty, delicious, and perfect for sharing.

Ingredients:

  • 4 hamburger buns with sesame seeds
  • 4 all-beef hamburger patties
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1⁄2 red onion
  • 1 medium dill pickle
  • 1 large tomatoes
  • 4 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 1⁄4 cup mustard
  • 1⁄4 cup ketchup

Steps:

  1. Grill the patties until they are almost cooked through.
  2. Add the cheese on top and cook until melted.
  3. Spread the mustard and ketchup on the buns.
  4. Put a patty on top of each.
  5. Add the lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and onion.
  6. Top with the top bun and serve.
