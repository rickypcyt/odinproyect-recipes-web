Regular Show Sandwich

Garfield loves lasagna more than anyone, and we developed this lasagna recipe drawing inspiration from the famous cartoon cat. As a result, we enjoyed a creamy, filling, and tasty dish. Lasagna is Garfield’s favorite dish, and we love it too. It’s filling, hearty, delicious, and perfect for sharing.

Ingredients:

  • Sandwich paper
  • Sliced ham
  • ½ pound of Ground beef
  • ⅓ cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and Pepper
  • ⅓ cup of BBQ sauce
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce
  • 1 large white bread sub sandwich bun

Steps:

  1. Mix the ricotta, parmesan, egg, and basil in a bowl. Add in the salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Spray a 13"x9" pan (2" deep) with cooking spray. Take the noodles and set them non-overlapping in the bottom of the pan. Spread half the cheese mixture over the noodles. Now spread some tomato sauce over the cheese. Sprinkle mozzarella on top. Repeat until you have a layer of noodles resting at the top of the pan, at which point you may add the remainder of the sauce and bake for 20 minutes.
  3. Let cool for ten minutes, then serve. (Note: This is not really intended for cats.).
