Garfield's

The Garfield's is the Krusty Krab's signature food item. It is a meatless hamburger of sorts made from seaweed-sea buns, undersea vegetables, condiments, and a patty; it can also be ordered with cheese.

Ingredients:

The Secret Formula:

  • 4 hamburger buns with sesame seeds
  • 4 all-beef hamburger patties
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1⁄2 red onion
  • 1 medium dill pickle
  • 1 large tomatoes
  • 4 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 1⁄4 cup mustard
  • 1⁄4 cup ketchup

Steps:

  1. Grill the patties until they are almost cooked through.
  2. Add the cheese on top and cook until melted.
  3. Spread the mustard and ketchup on the buns.
  4. Put a patty on top of each.
  5. Add the lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and onion.
  6. Top with the top bun and serve.
